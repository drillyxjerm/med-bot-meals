Mission Statement

Empowering nurses and caregivers by alleviating their workload, allowing them to focus on patient care. Our service ensures that patients in hospitals can effortlessly order and receive meals directly to their rooms, enhancing their comfort and recovery.

Our Goal

Our project involves a bot designed to deliver meals to patients, shortening the process for nurses to care for people more efficiently and promptly.
